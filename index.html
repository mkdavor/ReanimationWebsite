About Hands On Heart

Hands On Heart is a mobile app designed to educate and empower individuals to act during life-threatening emergencies. Our mission is to provide easy-to-follow guidance on reanimation and CPR techniques, helping you make a difference when it matters most.

Through this app, we aim to build confidence and competence, ensuring that more people are equipped to take lifesaving actions. Whether you’re a complete beginner or looking to refresh your skills, Hands On Heart is your trusted resource for learning how to save lives.

Support

At Hands On Heart, we are dedicated to supporting you in this vital journey of learning and preparedness. If you have any questions about using the app, need assistance, or would like to share feedback, our team is here to help.

We understand the importance of clarity and reliability, especially when dealing with life-saving knowledge. Please reach out to us with any concerns or inquiries, and we’ll do our best to assist you promptly.

You can contact us at support@hands-on.app. Your commitment to learning and helping others is invaluable, and we’re honored to support you every step of the way.
